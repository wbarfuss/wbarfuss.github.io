[**Complex System Modeling of Human-Environment Interactions**](https://wbarfuss.github.io/csm-of-hei/)  [Open-level course]  University of Bonn - *Running each winter term since 23/24*
> This semester-long course provides an integrated perspective on different modeling approaches (dynamics, equilibrium, agent-based) applied to human-environment interactions and sustainability transitions. Its scope is practical, utilizing Python within Jupyter Notebooks. Prerequisites are basic mathematical skills. The course material is available open source: [[lecture notes](https://wbarfuss.github.io/csm-of-hei/)] [[source](https://github.com/wbarfuss/csm-of-hei)]

**Systems Modeling: Environment, Economics, and Complexity** [PhD course] Center for Development Research (ZEF), University of Bonn - with Jan Börner, Daniel Callo-Concha, Cory Whitney - *Running each winter term since 24/25*
