Hi. I’m Wolf. I’m a research scientist working on the dynamics of collective learning.

I seek to uncover the fundamental principles of collective learning in changing environments for robust, cooperative and sustainable collective intelligence. To do so, I combine ideas from theoretical physics, complex systems, multi-agent learning, (evolutionary) game theory and social-ecology.

Research Scientist @ Tuebingen AI Center, Uni Tuebingen, GER (Bethge Lab)
Guest Researcher @ EEB, Princeton University, USA (Levin Lab)
Guest Researcher @ PIK Potsdam, GER (ERA Lab & WhESAgroup)
Member @ Earth and Resilience Initiative, Princeton-Stockholm-Potsdam
